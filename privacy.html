Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GrowthLog ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and application at growthlog.app.

1. Information We Collect

GrowthLog is designed with privacy by default. The core application stores all your data locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not have access to your goals, journal entries, reflections, or any other data you enter in the app.

We may collect the following non-personal data automatically:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent (via Google Analytics, if enabled)
  • Referral source (how you found us)
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)

2. How We Use Information

Any analytics data collected is used solely to understand how people use GrowthLog so we can improve the product. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies for:

  • Google Analytics (anonymised usage analytics)
  • Google AdSense (advertising, if applicable)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Our core application functions without cookies.

4. Google AdSense

We may display advertisements via Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Affiliate Links

Our website contains affiliate links to third-party platforms (such as Udemy, Coursera, and Blinkist). If you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. These links are always clearly indicated.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • Google Analytics — website usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense — advertising (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Netlify — website hosting (Netlify Privacy Policy)

7. Data Security

Since your journal data is stored locally in your browser, it never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. Clearing your browser data will permanently delete your GrowthLog entries.

8. Children's Privacy

GrowthLog is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated date. Continued use of GrowthLog after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@growthlog.app
